How it all started

I graduated with a major in Marketing but there was a time I wondered if I should go for the marketing industry after graduation. As a student I was lucky to get several part-time jobs in marketing agencies, and everyone told me that I was "fit", that I had prospect in the industry. However, when it came to the big question, "is this what you want to do for the rest of your life?," the answer was no. It's true that marketing interests me, but I was not really passionate about it.

So when I was a senior in college I got a job in a French IT company, as I wanted to work in the tech industry and I speak French. Over there I was working on network system projects, which were pretty foreign to me. Learning about network was tricky, but at the end of the day I knew that I found something that inspired me.
